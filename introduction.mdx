# Welcome to Audentic

Audentic empowers businesses to add natural voice interactions to their websites as easily as embedding a video. Our no-code platform enables you to create AI-powered voice agents that can handle everything from form filling to document navigation, transforming how users interact with your web content.

## Key Features

- **No-Code Integration**: Add voice AI to your website with a simple embed code
- **Natural Conversations**: Powered by state-of-the-art AI models for human-like interactions
- **Custom Voice Agents**: Design voice interactions tailored to your brand and use case
- **Cross-Browser Compatible**: Works seamlessly across all major web browsers
- **Enterprise-Ready**: Built with security, scalability, and reliability in mind

## Why Voice AI?

Voice is one of the most natural and efficient ways for humans to communicate. With recent breakthroughs in AI technology, we can now bring this natural interaction method to the web, making digital experiences more accessible and engaging than ever before.
