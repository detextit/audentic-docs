# Welcome to Audentic

Audentic enables businesses to add voice agents to their websites as easily as embedding a video, like the one above. Our no-code platform lets you bring the latest in voice and AI to your business. Let us handle the technical challenges, while you focus on building your brand.

## Why Voice AI?

Voice is one of the most natural and efficient ways for humans to communicate. With recent breakthroughs in AI technology, we can now bring this natural interaction method to the web, making digital experiences more accessible and engaging than ever before.
